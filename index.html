Bartek Ringwelski

Partner

Bartek Ringwelski is a venture capital investor and entrepreneur based in the New York City area. Previously, Bartek was a Product Manager at Facebook, where he incubated and launched payments on Instagram Direct and led Messenger Business Payments for the APAC market. His earlier experience includes serving as COO of bitFlyer USA, helping launch the US operations of Japan's largest crypto exchange, and working as a Partner at Core Innovation Capital, an early-stage fintech venture firm. Bartek holds an MBA from The Wharton School and a BA in Economics from Columbia University. Previously, he founded SkillSlate, which was acquired by TaskRabbit in 2013. He also taped a $2 bill to Bear Stearns HQ at the start of the financial crisis.

Kyle Libra is a seasoned operator with nearly 20 years of experience at early and growth-stage startups and based in the New York City area.
