Our Call for Founders

Gravity Labs turns innovative ideas into market realities. We develop theses based on market research, ecosystem conversations, and strategic partner needs — and when we identify opportunities, we actively seek founders who can bring these ideas to life.

If you're an experienced operator, a domain expert, a first-time founder, or a serial entrepreneur passionate about solving challenges in financial infrastructure, we want to hear from you.

Who We're Looking For

Operators with domain expertise

You've built products, shipped code, or scaled operations in crypto, fintech, or infrastructure. You understand the technical complexities and regulatory landscape.

Strategic thinkers who spot gaps

You've identified problems others haven't noticed yet. You see where the market is heading and what infrastructure needs to exist to get there.

Builders ready to commit

You're prepared to dedicate years to turning an idea into a category-defining company. You value partnership over independence and understand that great companies are built collaboratively.

Our Focus

Gravity Labs' expertise is in the future of financial infrastructure—and we strongly believe that future will be built onchain. While we remain opportunistic and thesis-flexible, we're particularly focused on incubating companies addressing these challenges.
