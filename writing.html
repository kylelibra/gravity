Our Writing

Where Do Good Startup Ideas Come From? Kyle Libra · January 23, 2026

This piece dismantles the myth of the “eureka moment” and reveals the surprisingly formulaic origins behind companies like Uber, Slack, and Airbnb. It includes two practical frameworks for evaluating startup ideas — one lightweight for side projects, one battle-tested at a venture studio — making it a useful read for anyone sitting on a list of half-baked ideas wondering which one to pursue.

The Race to be the New Everything Bank Kyle Libra · November 26, 2025

Fintech platforms and legacy banks are locked in a $300B race to become the “everything bank” through tokenization. The battlefield? Stablecoins. The stakes? 54% of JPMorgan’s quarterly revenue. Here’s what you need to know if you’re building in the crossfire.

A Lot of What People Think About Stablecoins Is Wrong Kyle Libra · September 16, 2025

Circle and Tether haven’t won—they’ve commoditized themselves. Here’s why the real opportunity for founders is in distribution and infrastructure, not issuance.

Reading List

The Retail Trade: Crypto vs Equities Wintermute · February 26, 2026

Wintermute’s data-driven analysis of how retail capital flows between crypto and equities have fundamentally shifted. As crypto volatility compresses with institutional ETF adoption, retail traders are increasingly treating stocks and digital assets as direct substitutes rather than correlated risk-on bets — with major implications for what drives the next crypto cycle.

From Adoption to Annexation Meltem Demirors · February 24, 2026

A sharp-eyed take on what institutional adoption of crypto actually looks like in practice. Demirors argues that institutions aren’t here to participate in the crypto economy — they’re here to turn it into a fee stream for their AUM machines. A useful counterweight to the uncritical “institutions are coming” narrative.

17 things we’re excited about for crypto in 2026 a16z crypto team · February 5, 2026

The a16z crypto team lays out the ideas, technologies, and trends they believe will define the next wave of crypto development — from AI-crypto intersections and on-chain governance to infrastructure improvements that could unlock mainstream adoption.

The AI revolution is here. Will the economy survive the transition? Michael Burry, Dwarkesh Patel, Patrick McKenzie, Jack Clark · January 9, 2026

The man who predicted the 2008 crash, Anthropic’s co-founder, and a leading AI podcaster jump into a Google Doc to debate the economic impact of AI — covering labor displacement, productivity gains, regulatory responses, and whether the transition will be orderly or catastrophic.

Perpetual futures, explained Patrick McKenzie · December 5, 2025

A deep, accessible breakdown of how perpetual futures work in crypto — the dominant trading instrument most people outside the industry have never heard of. McKenzie explains the mechanics, the funding rate system that keeps perps tethered to spot prices, and why a plurality of all stablecoins exist to collateralize these positions.

Crypto Trends Report, 2025 Edition Alana Levin · November 18, 2025

A comprehensive overview of the crypto landscape heading into 2025, covering where capital is flowing, which sectors are gaining momentum, and the macro and regulatory dynamics shaping the market. Essential context for founders and investors evaluating where to build next.
