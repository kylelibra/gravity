Almanax

AI-Powered Security for Web3

Almanax is building an AI security engineer that continuously detects, triages, and patches vulnerabilities across codebases. Founded by industry veterans from Coinbase, AnChain.AI, and Ripple, Almanax addresses the critical security challenges facing Web3 development—where over $9 billion has been stolen in the past three years, and 90% of hacked contracts had already been audited.

The platform uses large language models to analyze code, understand logic, and identify both classic exploits and subtle logic-level bugs that traditional static analysis tools miss. Integrated directly into CI/CD pipelines, Almanax functions like an automated security reviewer, catching vulnerabilities before they reach production.

While initially focused on smart contract security, Almanax has expanded to scan broader software layers including off-chain code and third-party dependencies, positioning itself as essential infrastructure for any team building in high-stakes environments.

Vault

DeFi Infrastructure for Institutional Capital

Vault is building programmable vault infrastructure that enables institutional-grade capital deployment in DeFi. As the decentralized finance ecosystem matures beyond shared lending pools into modular, risk-controlled strategies, Vault provides the transparent, auditable infrastructure that bridges traditional finance expectations with on-chain innovation.

The platform addresses a fundamental limitation that has kept institutions out of DeFi: the inability to maintain control, visibility, and risk management over digital assets while accessing yield opportunities. Vault's approach allows capital allocators to deploy assets across lending, liquidity provision, and tokenized real-world assets through smart contracts that enforce clear mandates, provide real-time transparency, and maintain strict access controls.
